Superclass & Subclass - Parent & Child
The program must accept the name and age of two persons(parent and child) as the input. The program must print the name and age of the 
two persons after X years as the output. The value of X is also passed as the input to the program.

Please fill in the missing lines of code in the class Child so that the program runs successfully.

Example Input/Output 1:
Input:
Hector
50
Gavin
23
10

Output:
Hector
60
Gavin
33

Explanation:
The name of the parent is Hector and his age is 50.
The name of the child is Gavin and his age is 23.
After 10 years, the age of Hector will be 60 and the age of Gavin will be 33.
Hence the output is
Hector
60
Gavin
33

Input:
Arun
30
Bhuvana
4
18

Output:
Arun
48
Bhuvana
22
